About this App

This is a Windows app for the content of the Kingdom Hearts Wiki.

The app is built upon the Wikipedia Windows app. The code for this app is 100% open source, and is available on the app page on the wiki. If you have any additional knowledge on coding and wish to contribute, contact KeybladeSpyMaster.

Special thanks to Brion Vibber and the folks at the Wikimedia and Wikipedia Moblie Apps Team!

About the Kingdom Hearts Wiki

The Kingdom Hearts Wiki is an independently-hosted wiki that focuses on providing the most professional and complete information on the Kingdom Hearts series. The wiki was founded on April 1st, 2006 at Wikia, and moved on February 9th, 2011 to an independent server.

The Kingdom Hearts Wiki is entirely built by fans like you!

The wiki is not affiliated in any way with Square Enix Holdings, LTD, the Walt Disney Company, or any of their subsidiaries or affiliated companies.
