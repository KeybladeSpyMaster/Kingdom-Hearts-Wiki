Help

Features

Share

Share your favorite articles to your friends. Just use the Share option in Charms to share to Facebook, Twitter, or anyother connected apps.

Search

Search the entire Kingdom Hearts Wiki. Just start typing anywhere on the app, or use the Search option in Charms.

Actions

Right-click in the content area or swipe from the bottom to access additional actions, like "Pin to Start", "Read in Browser", and "Find in Page."

History

Go back through your current session. Just right-click or hold on the back button in the Header to see all your recent pages.

Main Page

Check out what's up at the wiki! Just search for "Main Page" to access the wiki's Main Page.

Please Note

  • Do not click on the "Edit" links. The links do not currently work, and will only lead to blank pages. I hope to eventually bring editing functionality.
  • Do not use anything in "Options". The fly-out is a placeholder for future features (hopefully).
  • Music files and videos, including the Wiki's Podcast and Let's Play, do not currently play in-app, nor do they link to the YouTube videos in browser. I hope to bring this functionality soon.
  • The Wiki's Magazine does not currently display perfectly because of limitations on gradients. However, it's still mostly readable.
  • Anchor links do not currently work. They open the right page but do not skip to the anchored section.
